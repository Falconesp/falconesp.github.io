Fashion and Food 7 Days Tour in Milan
Milano is one of the most stylish places in the world! We'll take a stroll together around the Fashion District, or as the Milanese like to call it, the "Quadrilatero della Moda", discovering a select number of noble family homes, and understanding how they used to live. The tour is completed by enjoying specialty food tastings in some of the city's best shops and bakeries, ending in true Milanese style with a typical Italian aperitivo at the historic bar of Camparino which is located at the entrance to the stunning Vittorio Emanuele II Gallery. Any "Milanese" worth his salt knows this area like the back of his hand, so join me on this tour to feel like a real adopted "Milanese"!
